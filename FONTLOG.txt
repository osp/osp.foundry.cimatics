
FONTLOG
Cimatics
==============


This file provides detailed information on the Cimatics font software.
This information should be distributed along with the Cimatics fonts and any derivative works.

Basic Font Information
--------------------------
Cimatics was designed by OSP (http://ospublish.constantvzw.org) in July 2009 for the graphic identity of Cimatics A\V Platform (http://www.cimatics.com). 
It is based on URW Gothic L.
Copyright (URW)++, Copyright 1999 by (URW)++ Design & Development; Cyrillic glyphs added by Valek Filippov (C) 2001-2005

Cimatics gathers glyphs from FreeSerif, FreeSerifItalic, DejaVuSans, DejaVuSerif, joined by the OSP_frog mascot, the Cimatics two piece heart, a baronchon_palm_tree from Open Clip Art Library (www.openclipart.org) and private use dingbats drawned for Cimatics (CimaticsScaredEye, CimaticsWhitePentagon). 


Cimatics currently provides the following Unicode coverage:
Basic Latin: 95/95 
Latin-1 Supplement: 93/96
Latin Extended-A: 128/128
Latin Extended-B: 3/208
Cyrillic: 198/256
